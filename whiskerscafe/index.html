Our Story

Founded in 2005, Whiskers Cafe was built to share the love for food and drinks as well as our passion for animal welfare.

We realised that the chaotic streets of Manchester needed a place for people to retreat and unwind as they connect with our animals in a relaxing environment.

Our Mission - Every Cat Deserves A Home

Our primary goal is to find our felines new homes through the help of our customers. We ensure they have a temporary safe-space while they await adoption.
