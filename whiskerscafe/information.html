Visitor Information

Everything you need to know to help you have a safe and enjoyable visit.

Cafe Rules

  • Be gentle and remain calm around our animals.
  • Do not attempt to pick up or move the cats without permission.
  • Do not feed the cats any food unless approved by staff.
  • Children must be under supervision at all times.

Booking Information

  • Visits should be booked in advance on our website.
  • Please arrive on time for your allocated timeslot.
  • Contact a member of staff prior to the date if you wish to change or cancel your booking.
Book A Visit

Accessibility

  • Our staff is trained to support visitors with accessibility needs.
  • Please contact a member of staff before visiting the cafe if extra support is required.
  • If you have any accessibility queries and/or suggestions (in-person or on the website), please email our support team.

FAQs

Can I adopt a cat?

Yes, a range of cats at our cafe are available for adoption. Please visit their profiles on the Our Cats page for more information.

Can I bring my own cat?

No, outside pets may disturb the other animals and therefore banned from the cafe.

Can I feed the cats?

Only approved treats may be used, it must be within a feeding limit (per cat), and staff must provide permission first.
